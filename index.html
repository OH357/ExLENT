About the Program

What is ExLENT?

ExLENT — Engaging East Tennessee Educators in Microelectronics — is a University of Tennessee, Knoxville initiative designed to bridge the gap between the growing demand for microelectronics talent and the lack of access to relevant education in Tennessee's high schools.

ExLENT is not just a curriculum — it's a pipeline for talent, a training ground for teachers, and a launchpad for students into the future of microelectronics and technology innovation.

Through hands-on classroom kits, structured teacher professional development, and direct student engagement, ExLENT prepares Tennessee's future engineers, innovators, and leaders for high-demand careers in semiconductor and microelectronics industries.

Together, we will ensure every student — regardless of background — has access to the tools and knowledge that will define tomorrow's economy.

Program Details

Built for Students & Educators

ExLENT offers tailored pathways for every participant in the learning journey.

Build. Invent. Launch Your Future.

Microelectronics is one of the fastest-growing industries in the world. Tennessee is at the center of this transformation — and ExLENT puts you at the center of it.

Whether you're into coding, engineering, design, or innovation, microelectronics opens doors to incredible careers.

  • Program microcontrollers and build real working devices
  • Design IoT devices and sensor-based projects
  • Learn soldering and circuit assembly fundamentals
  • Explore robotics and wearable electronics
  • Connect with UTK engineers and industry professionals
  • Discover high-wage, high-demand STEM career paths
01

Discover & Explore

Start with block-based coding and simple circuits. No prior experience required.

02

Build & Prototype

Advance to intermediate microcontrollers. Build real working devices, sensors, and IoT projects.

03

Innovate & Present

Design original projects. Explore robotics and advanced electronics for deeper engineering challenges.

04

Launch Your Career

Connect with UTK faculty, mentors, and industry partners for internships and degree pathways.

Bring Microelectronics to Your Classroom.

Microelectronics is one of the most in-demand fields of the 21st century — and your students deserve access. ExLENT gives you everything you need to teach it confidently.

No prior electronics experience required. UTK trains you, equips your classroom, and supports you every step of the way.

  • Summer professional development training at UTK
  • Ready-to-use lesson plans and lab activities
  • Full classroom equipment kit — your school keeps it
  • Ongoing support from UTK faculty and graduate students
  • Stipends and continuing education credits
  • Network of fellow ExLENT educators across East Tennessee

Classroom Equipment

Three Tiers of Classroom Kits

ExLENT delivers professionally curated equipment kits directly to participating classrooms — at no cost to the school. Each kit is matched to the teacher's training level and school readiness.

Entry Level
Starter Kit
Ideal for introducing coding and basic electronics with no prior experience required.
  • Entry-level microcontroller boards for every student team
  • Block-based and text-based programming activities
  • Basic measurement and testing tools
  • Guided lesson plans and project booklets
  • UTK teacher training and support included
Most Popular
Intermediate Kit
A complete hands-on STEM lab experience for classrooms ready to go deeper into electronics.
  • Intermediate microcontroller and IoT platforms
  • Sensor arrays for real-world data collection projects
  • Soldering and analog electronics labs
  • Shared diagnostic and testing equipment
  • Consumables, components, and safety supplies
  • Full curriculum and professional development
Advanced
Full Kit
For advanced CTE or honors programs ready for college-level engineering exploration.
  • All Intermediate kit components included
  • Robotics platforms for autonomous systems
  • Wearable electronics and e-textile projects
  • Programmable logic and advanced development boards
  • Radio frequency and spectrum analysis tools
  • Professional-grade lab instruments

Equity & Inclusion

Microelectronics for Everyone

Tennessee students — regardless of ZIP code, background, or prior experience — deserve access to the careers shaping our future. ExLENT is built around this commitment. We actively partner with schools serving underrepresented populations and design every program element to remove barriers to participation.

👧

Gender Inclusion

Intentional outreach and role modeling to encourage young women into microelectronics.

🌍

Rural Access

Priority partnerships with rural East Tennessee schools historically underserved by STEM programs.

🎨

STEAM Integration

E-textile and wearable projects bridge technology with creative and artistic learning.

🤝

Community Partnerships

Engaging local industry, community colleges, and school boards as program champions.
